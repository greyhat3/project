Our Vision

The FutureSpec project was creatd in order to bridge the gap between experienced stock traders and ordinary people with an interest in stock trading essentially bridging the metaphorical Wall street divide.

Our Mission

We provide an easier way to make predictions than simple going with a gut feeling or having to spend a lot of time calculating manually. We believe in making prdictions from verifiable data rather that human opinion which can be biased, bought or simply uneducated.

We are first and foremost dedicated to producing the best quality predictions in order to give the best possible advice we can to our users.
